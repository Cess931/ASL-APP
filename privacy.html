Introduction

Welcome to Basic ASL App. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your personal information when you use our app.

We may collect and process the following data about you:

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our ASL learning services

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:
