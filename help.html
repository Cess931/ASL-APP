Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a Practice? +
To start a Practice, go to the 'Practice' page, select a mode, and press Play. You can choose difficulty to match your level.
Can I study offline? +
No. The site does not currently have a fully offline mode.
How is my data used? +
We only use limited data for site performance. Visit the Privacy page for full details and how to control your settings.
